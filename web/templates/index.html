Your AI-powered travel assistant for finding the fastest, cheapest, or shortest routes between cities.

How It Works

Our planner uses advanced graph-based algorithms like Dijkstra, BFS, and DFS to calculate optimal travel routes. Whether you want to minimize cost, distance, or travel time, we’ve got you covered.

  • 📍 Select your start and destination cities
  • ⏱️ Choose your priority: fastest, cheapest, or shortest route
  • 📊 Get a clear route visualization and travel summary

Why Use This Planner?

  • 🔹 Accurate shortest-path calculations
  • 🔹 Handles multiple constraints like cost, distance, and time
  • 🔹 Open-source and easy to customize
  • 🔹 Ideal for students, travelers, and logistics enthusiasts
